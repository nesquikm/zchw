# Assignment: Zencoder Homework

Source: https://forgoodai.notion.site/assignment-zencoder

## Task

For an imaginary product that allows engineers to run agents in the cloud (e.g., https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-code-on-the-web), vibe-code the production implementation of a customer-facing organizational-level analytics dashboard around their use of such agents.

You decide what metrics are important and should be shown in the dashboard.

> Mock any APIs or data you need as necessary in the vibe coded solution.

## Guidance

1. Use any open-source project(s)/code you want to jump-start
2. Use an AI-first and AI-everywhere approach to help you:
   1. Deep research on the scope/spec/etc
   2. Develop wireframes/prototypes
   3. Develop the code
   4. Stub data/APIs
3. For the code itself, use AI-First spec-driven development:
   1. Requirements spec
   2. Technical implementation spec
   3. Testing spec (for TDD and acceptance testing)
   4. Step-by-step plan
   5. Agentic execution

> We will provide Claude Max subscription so you are not hitting the token limits. Please use the email provided by your recruiter to log in to Claude Code. Then, check your personal inbox for a magic link to approve your access.

## Acceptance

We will review the output together in two separate interviews (mostly on the final one):

- **Second Interview:** a bit of a focus on technical decisions such as the tech stack, structure, etc.
- **Final Interview:** review your AI-first experience and product decisions with the CEO and Founder.

Please share a link to your repository one day before the interview so we can review it.

## Pay attention to the quality of the human decisions you made

- How valuable and usable is the solution you designed
- How good is your test coverage

## Effort

This is an open-ended task — you can invest as much or as little effort as you want.

Our recommendation: invest enough to show your talent and skills.
